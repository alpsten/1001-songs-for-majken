Ground Control to Major Tom. Count down. Ignition. And then the quiet of space.

Bowie recorded this in 1969, weeks before the moon landing. Whether that timing was planned or accidental hardly matters — the song arrived exactly when the world was ready to imagine leaving Earth behind.

Major Tom does not panic. He floats. He looks at Earth from far away and tells someone he loves them. That is the whole of it.

I included this song because I want you to be willing to float sometimes. To go somewhere strange and unfamiliar and stay calm enough to notice how beautiful it looks from out there.
