Bowie wrote Heroes in Berlin at a time when the city was divided in two. The wall was real. The guards were real. The separation was real. And yet somehow the song is not about despair. It is about two people standing at the wall and deciding that, just for today, they are kings and queens.

That is the whole argument of the song: that even within limits, even within circumstances you did not choose, you can have something that is entirely yours.

I think about this song when the world feels too large and too complicated. It is a reminder that you do not need to win everything. Sometimes one moment is enough.
