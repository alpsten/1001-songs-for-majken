Billy Joel wrote this based on his own experience playing bars in Los Angeles before he became famous. The people in the song — the bartender, the waitress, the old man — are all real. All of them are waiting for something.

What I love about this song is that it does not pity them. It witnesses them. The piano man plays, they sing along, and for a few minutes they are all in the same place at the same time. That is no small thing.

There will be moments in your life when the place you are is not the place you imagined. This song is for those moments. Find the piano. Sing along.
